The cable break should be followed by high stakes negotiations with the megacorps.  Since the corps need a neutral party, the player can insist on conditions before agreeing to enforce the peace.  Pushing a hard bargain should anger the corporations with different terms angering different corps or all of them.

Some conditions to include:

Put the corporate police in Docks/Railyard/Vats under city jurisdiction
If all three of those are already chosen, you can also demand limelight which would really annoy brazos

Move police under city jurisdiction from corporate to city payrolls at:
-Same payscale (very expensive) (10 times poverty level)
-City payscale (2 times poverty level)
-6 times poverty level
-6 times poverty level with city police given a raise to match (even more expensive)

Institute a corporate tax to fund the police.  You can demand revenues up to the amount the police will cost with the jurisdiction negotiation.  This tax could be applied to all three or Brazos could be exempt (since they aren't fighting).  Brazos is annoyed if not exempt and very annoyed if not exempt and you aren't taking over their police.  The other two are annoyed if Brazos is exempt.

Allow the government to start enforcing civil rights laws again (would unlock the relevent card)

Insist on city judges running trials for corporate mercs who violate the ceasefire
Have city trials with a panel of 3 corporate and 2 city judges.
Either of the previous two would allow you to demand a review of the city judges under a review panel with 3 corporate picked evaluators and either 2 or 4 city picked evaluators

Instead of taking over the corporate police you could also demand that they be disbanded and replaced with city police or disbanded and replaced with militia.  If replaced with city police, you could demand funding.


If you had any leverage on a corporation before the cable break scene, you could also demand things that aren't security related:

Enroll the children of their employees in city education.
Pay for that education.

Start paying the land tax on their holdings in order to fund the government general revenues

At the start of these negotiations you would get a big amount of leverage on all three corporations (in addition to any existing leverage).  They will agree as long as leverage exceeds their reprisal score.  You need to make an agreement that two of them are willing to vote for.  (And if the 3rd has reprisal exceed leverage, they will start hitting you.)
