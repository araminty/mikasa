There should be major events which serve as a change in the dynamic of the game:

Phase 0 prelude (probably cut this) 
decide what faction to play, get historical stuff

Phase 1 organizing/aurora
Initially you only have any power in aurora and you are laying the groundwork for later.
Ending 1: If you mess up the organizing, your party becomes irrelevent and the game ends as other parties take the lead

Crisis 1: The tether snap
Suddenly the Corps are at each other's throats and you have a chance to start exercising power citywide.

Phase 2: The corpo war
You are mostly a neutral party in the corporate war but you need to manage the violence/crime/anarchy it causes, gaining power as you do
Ending 2: If you dont build up power enough during the corpo war to have a seat at the table then when it simmers down they freeze you out again and the game ends.

Crisis 2: The attack on Titan followed by the Kipple Crisis followed by the Great Protector
These events happen in rapid succession, starting the waves of refugees from Jupiter, Earth and otherworld

Phase 3: Managing the crisis, keeping xenophobes in check
In this phase you need to address a severe strain on the infrastructure, with the housing supply serving as an overall proxy.  You need to manage the corporations to have the resources to address the crisis.  The xenophobes will steadily gain power which will intensify when there is popular (not elite) backlash to your actions or if the homelessness rate gets too high.  As their power passes certain threshhold events fire where they do evil shit.
Ending 3: If the xenophobes become too powerful, they make a deal with the syndicates and take over, cutting you out.

Crisis 4: The end of the refugee crisis means the corporations dont need you anymore.  They start sabotaging you with unreasonable demands.  The way you play this leads to alternate outcomes, none of which has a set timetable:
Crisis 4a: The corpos decide it's time to crack down on you leading to you needing to either give up or rebel
Crisis 4b: you manage the corporation demands well enough that they decide to tolerate you and instead crack down on the syndicates and xenophobes and they decide that crackdown is your job.
Crisis 4c: Or maybe otherworld demons got a little too interested in Mikasa and decide to invade ("wholesome" ending where socialists, corporates, democrats and fascists all unite to fight deamons)

Phase 4: Have your big showdown
