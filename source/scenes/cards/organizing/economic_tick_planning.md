Corporate strength:
    determines relative power (including relative to outside corps)
    ticks upwards with spending
    ticks downwards with disorder and intercorporate violence
    ticks downwards with syndicate strength
    outside corp strength soley based on trade level
    corporate exports are trade level time strength
    corporate domestic scales very small fraction of mikasa wealth
    ticks downwards with sabotage ability (unless in truce?)

Spending
    Corporations spend a fraction of cash on increasing power
        Share of this goes local as local industry increases
    drones spend a fraction of cash, trade level determines local/import split
    coops spend a fraction of cash on increasing power
        Share of this goes local as coop market and industry scores increase
    affluent/poor spend fraction of cash


Check export revenues:
    Corporations get export revenues based on their absolute strength
    Coops get small amount of export revenues based on trade levels (not industrialization)
    longshore revenues based on trade level squared
        -corps/drones get lions share, coops, poor and affluent get some
    tourism revenues base on trade level times disorderly crime factor
        -corps/affluent get lions share, coops and poor get some
    
Check import spending:
    corporate/drone cash with trade level factor
    coop import spending shift towards local spending as markets and industrialization progress
    coop, afluent and poor share of spending on imports rises as cash reserves rise
